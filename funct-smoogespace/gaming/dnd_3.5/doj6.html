Description

To many elves, Doj Starwatcher is way too young to have left the motherlands.

To peoples not familiar with how the elves look, he looks like most other elves with

long black hair, ceder green eyes, and the thin face of the high elves. His voice is soft

and has the cadences that one brought up in a priests family may expect.

People who spend time with Doj realize that he is much younger than he admits and

much more trusting and naieve than the elf of 140 years he claims to be.


Personal History

Doj Starwatcher was born to an upper class family of priests.

His father, Colnor, was a 'strict' diciple of self reliance and personal

freedom and expected his son to follow in his footsteps. His mother

taught more inclusiveness and peacemaking, but her family was known for

being a bit off.. with adventurers every generation and a slur that one

of their ancestors was a human.

His mothers brother, Ta'erno was one of those adventurers and on his

rare visits introduced Doj to other species and worldviews. Ta'erno's

regular companions were a couple of humans and a dwarf who wore silver.

Colnor did not approve since from his viewpoint humans and dwarves were

the cause of the gods leaving and the present troubles. Elves should

stay within their borders, focus on surviving until the gods returned,

and build a new world when the 'defilers' had faced justice. [A rather

extreme view, but one that Doj's clan advocated.]

Doj found that the elven priesthood wasnt his calling, and his fathers

views were definately too closed in for what Doj saw as the good of

elfdom. He didnt seem to have the native nack for majic that others did,

and only could access it through much study and contemplation. On one

visit from his uncle, one of the human companions told him that this was

normal for humans and that there were schools to learn magic

methodically versus natively. The old wizard (who had been much younger

when Doj remembered him last) gave him an old spellbook and some

supplies before heading off with Ta'erno to quest for an ancient elf

item maker. [Uncle Ta'erno and the wizard Bean actually stayed for 8

years in the area while Doj apprenticed with him.. but it seems such a

short time.]

After many family fights and as the time for his majority arrived, his

father told him to make a decision to follow the elven ways he taught or

lose his family name. Doj chose to follow his own path, and was

'banished' from his family. As he was leaving the forests he had always

known, he met a caravan that was heading south in search for a magical

fruit that cured all diseases.

Unknown to Doj, His mother has sent word to her brother to find Doj and

bring him back to try and patch things up. Whether this occurs or not..

is a sidepoint...

What this leads to is that Doj is disappointed in the Elven gods (or at

least the way his father taught them). He is looking for a focus of

faith but doesnt know where to look. The Human god that Panza follows is

definately not for him... and the view of Taibn through his companions

eyes have not helped him.


Adventure Journal

The first recorded adventures of Doj 'Shadowlord' were those helping companions in the sunken citidal. There he helped prove himself useful with a bow taken from the dead goblins, and with a sword. He also had a knack for wanting to avoid battles that seems somewhat of a phobia.

The next adventures showed how Doj and his companions brought the bodies to their family. The family requested that a magic apple be fed to both of them... sadly it was not known what horrors this would create. The companions then left to the north to follow clues to an ancient dwarven forge who had created metals quite rare. In between he found that he could not protect everyone, and that his magic had limits.. for 2 old people who gave shelter to his party under some duress lost their lives to something very evil that walked the nights. Travelling north, Doj found that he was not good with alcohol and spent a week recovering from its poison.

Getting to Blazingdale, Doj continued to follow his friends lead, and soon found himself earning the wrath of the town for bringing another ancient evil to them. There too he learned that his companions could die when the might Eorc was felled by an evil undead cleric. Travelling across the lands to find a priest who could possibly raise Eorc, he learnt the nature of various human religions. From there the party searched the hills around Blazingdale to find the ancient Dwarven keep. After many battles, and wars they were able to nearly clear it of the evil that inhabited it. There the young elf fell in lust with an evil black dragon, and may have somehow not given his all when the battle needed it.

Travelling from the keep to a wintering hole to the south, the party encountered more evil, and Doj began to see the consequences of his actions from the past. Helping to clean out the town of an evil mind-flayer, he began to wonder if his failures were from running away or not giving himself completely to some task. It was time to grow up and help the people who lived outside the magic lands he had grown up in.
